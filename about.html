About Us

Little Abqari Preschool: Nurturing Young Minds.

stimulating learning environment that lays a strong
foundation for your child's future success.
We understand the importance of early childhood development
and strive to create a holistic approach to learning.
We offer a comprehensive curriculum that integrates play-based activities.
